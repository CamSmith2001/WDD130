How to Write Your First Resume

A resume is a short document that shows employers your skills, education, and experience. Even if you haven't had a job before, you can still create a strong resume

What do I Include?

1. Contact Information

Include:

  • Full Name
  • Email Address
  • Phone Number
  • LinkedIn (optional)

2. Education

List:

  • School Name
  • Degree or Program
  • Expected Graduation Date

3. Skills

Examples:

  • Communication
  • Teamwork
  • Time Management
  • Microsoft Office
  • Basic programming

4. Experience

If you haven't had a job yet, include:

  • School Projects
  • Volunteer work
  • Clubs or organizations
  • Leadership roles

Quick Resume Tips:

  • Keep it one to two pages
  • Use clear headings
  • Avoid spelling mistakes
  • Use simple formatting
